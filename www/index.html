Card Guide

Objective

Be the first player to get rid of all your cards. The last player still holding cards is the Shithead — the loser of the round.

Card Phases

You play through three layers of cards in order:

① Hand
3 cards held
→
② Face-up
3 visible cards
→
③ Blind
3 mystery cards

Before the first turn you may swap any hand card with any face-up card to optimise your setup.

How to Play

  • Play a card equal to or higher than the top card of the discard pile.
  • If you can't (or won't) play, pick up the entire pile — it joins your hand.
  • After playing from your hand, draw back up to 3 cards while the deck lasts.
  • You may play multiple cards of the same rank in a single turn.
  • The player holding the lowest card goes first.

Special Cards

2
2 — Wild Reset

Can be played on anything. Resets the pile — next player can play any card.

3
3 — Skip

Can be played on anything. Skips the next player's turn. Multiple 3s skip multiple players.

7
7 — Lower

Next player must play a 7 or lower. Reverses the usual "higher or equal" rule.

8
8 — Transparent

Invisible wild. The pile constraint stays the same as the card beneath the 8.

9
9 — Reverse

Reverses the direction of play. Multiple 9s cancel each other out.

10
10 — Wild Burn

Can be played on anything. Burns the entire pile out of the game. Same player goes again.

4×
Four of a Kind — Burn

Four cards of the same rank on top of the pile (across any turns) auto-burns the pile. The triggering player goes again.

Swap Phase

At the start of the game, swap hand cards with face-up cards freely. Your face-up cards are the last line of defence before hitting blind cards — put your strongest cards there.

Strong face-up candidates: 2 (wild), 10 (burn), Ace, King.
